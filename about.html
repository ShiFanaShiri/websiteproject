Who We Are?

Founded in 2014, Niveosys is an IT consulting and software development company based in Calicut(Kozhikode). We have already delivered our business IT solutions and other services to customers all around. Our professional team is dedicated to act instantly for providing customized software solutions according to customer requirements. The team of Niveosys will combine professional expertise with leading technologies to build better solutions for the future. Our team is available in India, Middle East, and the USA.

What makes Niveosys unique is its client collaborative approach followed in every project. This is why Niveosys has served a wide range of verticals including banking, retail, healthcare, education, manufacturing, construction, service centers, textiles, hotels, and more in a short time span.

Why Us?

As a software development company, we have the responsibility to provide the best solution to the customers. Over these years, we developed software for different industry domains and always get praised for the quality delivered and timely support. We offer simple, user-friendly, and error-free solutions with leading technology and attractive UI. The aim of Niveosys is to deliver maximum customer satisfaction and we always keep our software up-to-date by adding new features.
